Norma Miller

Norma Miller is the owner of Smooth Transitions of Boise, a full service relocation concierge that helps people transition from one home to another. She established Smooth Transitions of Boise after a career in project management, landscape architecture and real estate.

Norma's personal experience with her father’s move from their cherished family home, while residing in a different state, she realized the importance of helping this age group transition.

As a Senior Move Manager, Norma’s specialty is helping older adults and their families through the emotional and physical aspects of moving. Norma is honored to assist you and your family with the emotional and physical aspects of your move.

Norma is a proud member of the National Association of Senior Move Managers (NASMM).

Smooth Transitions

Barbara Morris launched Smooth Transitions in Louisville, Kentucky in 1995, and has sixty licensees from twenty-three states, Canada and New Zealand. Morris is the author of Moving for Seniors: A step-by-step workbook and a founding member of the NASMM. www.movingforseniors.com

NASMM

NASMM is a non profit professional association of companies whose goals are to help older adults and their families with all aspects of moving, including physical and emotional. NASMM is committed to providing the compassion, respect, and independence to seniors.
