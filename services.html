Household Downsizing - Move Management - Estate Dispersal

We are a full service Move Management/Coordination company. We help clients with all or most parts of moving, including planning and preparing for the move, developing floor plans, scheduling and overseeing movers, handling donations, shipping items to family members, arranging for storage, and assist in determining the best method of profitably disposing of unwanted items, packing, unpacking and completely resettling you in your new home.

Smooth Transitions also provides services for the dispersal of household belongings after the loss of a loved one. A loss introduces a new set of responsibilities, from removing personal belongings to the complete shutdown of an established home. Daily responsibilities or distances make these tasks seem even more overwhelming.

Smooth Transitions can help make these tasks feasible and timely. Working under the direction of family members, attorneys or trust officers, we organize and distribute the estate according to your and your family member’s wishes.

For out-of-town family members, Smooth Transitions provides peace of mind that someone is coordinating your family member’s affairs with the personal attention you would give.
