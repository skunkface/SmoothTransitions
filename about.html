We are a residential and senior relication service, specializing in helping older adults and their families through the daunting process of downsizing and moving to a new residence.

Our goal is to make your transition as smooth and stress free and possible, weather it's advising you or managing the entire project. We're happy to do as little or as much as needed.

Owner Norma Miller is industry licensed, insured and a proud member of NASMM, the National Association of Senior Move Managers. Norma's background in real estate and project management, along with training from Smooth Transitions' founder Barbara Morris, uniquely qualifies her to deal with the challenges of a move.
